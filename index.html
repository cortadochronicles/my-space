Gabriella's Blurbs

About me:

I'm a final semester senior and first gen student at UTampa majoring in Communication, Media, and Culture Studies. After I graduate in December, I will be applying to grad school with hopes of getting a Master's in food systems or gastronomy. Ultimately, the dream is to have a travel-based career writing or creating multi-media packages which bridge cross cultural gaps in the world.

I grew up cooking with my family and my love for food has grown with me into my adulthood, so my focus is in food and culture. If not a colada, I love a good oat milk cortado. Any weather, any time. In the last few years, I've steered toward a plant based diet and am interested in sustainable food systems.

My one true love would have to be live music. Concerts are my absolute favorite place to be; I've traveled to a plethora of places to see some of my favorite artists. Boston, Atlanta, Miami, Orlando, Nashville, and New York to name a few. I'm also a huge fan of sports: Indycar, NFL, NHL, F1. My Sundays are usually dedicated to one or all of the aforementioned sports. In other free time, I read, paint, write, and watch movies and shows.

That's a little (or a lottle) about me, and I look forward to getting to know you. I didn't even really mention my love for movies, shows, and books; but, my favorites can be seen to the left. Also, I am a firm believer in the oxford comma.

Who I'd like to meet:

Anyone. I'm a versatile and low maintenence indivdual so as long as we connect on some intellectual level, I'd say we can be friends. Let's grab a coffee or go to a concert!
